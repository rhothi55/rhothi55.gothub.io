Hiking in Norway

Trying to get in shape? Trying to have fun while doing it? Norway has you covered. Norway has a jaw dropping 5,853 hiking trails, with something for everyone, from old souls to young daredevils.

These trails are all great but some of the top rated ones is Pulpit Rock, Kjeragbolten, as well as Segla. But don't limit yourselves to our recommendations, most if not all of the trails of Norway will tickle your sense of adventure!
